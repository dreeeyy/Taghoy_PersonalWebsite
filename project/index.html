About Me

I am a 4-year student at the Jumalon Ballet Center and an apprentice of the Ballet Philippines. I am also a part of Cathedral Of Praise's Christian Arts Department ensemble. I did Rent's Maureen Johnson, a bisexual performance artist and Joanne Jefferson's girlfriend. I also did Rumpleteazer of Cats the Musical who specialize in petty theft and mischief and Mungojerrie's partner in crime and I am the understudy of Demeter.
